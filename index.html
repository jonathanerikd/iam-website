About the firm

PT Indo Artha Mandiri is an architecture and interior design practice based in Jakarta, North Jakarta. The studio works across architecture, interior design, and landscape — delivering projects that are rigorously designed, precisely documented, and built to endure.

Our portfolio spans religious and civic architecture, high-end residential design, office interiors for multinational clients, and landscape installations for government institutions. Every project begins from a single premise: design should serve its users completely.

The studio has built a reputation for high-quality 3D visualisation and design documentation, enabling clients to fully understand and commit to a design before a single wall is built. From concept through construction, we remain present at every stage.
